## Design Context

### Users
Job seekers, students, graduates, and professionals using the app to build a resume quickly while seeing a live preview.

### Brand Personality
Editorial, confident, warm. The interface should feel focused and professional without becoming cold or corporate.

### Aesthetic Direction
Editorial warm light theme: cream paper surfaces, aubergine ink, terracotta primary actions, amber highlights, and quiet clay borders. The app should feel like a polished writing desk for career documents rather than a generic purple dashboard.

### Design Principles
- Make the resume feel like the hero by using paper-inspired surfaces and restrained accents.
- Use warm, confidence-building colors instead of loud violet gradients.
- Keep forms calm and readable with clear focus states and high contrast.
- Use color sparingly for action and orientation, not decoration.
